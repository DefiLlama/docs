# RWA Token Submission Form

## 1. Basic Information

#### Logo

**Definition:**\
Official project or token logo.

**What to provide:**

* Direct image URL (PNG or SVG preferred)
* Minimum 200×200 resolution
* Transparent background preferred

**Example:**\
[`https://icons.llamao.fi/icons/protocols/ondo-finance?w=48&h=48`](https://icons.llamao.fi/icons/protocols/ondo-finance?w=48\&h=48)

**Answer:**

***

#### Ticker

**Definition:**\
Unique trading symbol of the token.

**What to provide:**

* 3–8 uppercase letters
* Must match onchain and exchange listings

**Example:**\
`USYC`

**Answer:**

***

#### Token Name

**Definition:**\
Full official name of the tokenized asset.

**Example:**\
`Ondo US Dollar Yield Token`

**Answer:**

***

#### Website

**Definition:**\
Official project website.

**Example:**\
`https://ondo.finance`

**Answer:**

***

#### Twitter / X Profile

**Definition:**\
Official Twitter/X account of the project or issuer.

**Example:**\
`https://x.com/ondofinance`

**Answer:**

***

#### Telegram OR Discord

**Definition:**\
Official community or team communication channel.

**What to provide:**\
Telegram group/channel OR Discord invite link.

**Example:**\
`https://t.me/(projectname)`\
OR\
`https://discord.gg/(projectname)`

**Answer:**

***

## 2. Blockchain Information

#### Primary Chain

**Definition:**\
Main blockchain where the canonical version of the token is issued.

**Example:**\
`ethereum`

**Answer:**

***

#### All Chains

**Definition:**\
All blockchains where the token exists.

**Example:**\
`ethereum, arbitrum, base`

**Answer:**

***

#### Contracts

**Definition:**\
Official token contract address(es).

**What to provide:**\
Chain-prefixed format:

`chain:contract_address`

**Example:**\
`ethereum:0x1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef12345678`

**Answer:**

***

#### Internal Addresses

**Definition:**\
Issuer-controlled addresses used for treasury, minting, custody, or asset management.

**What to provide:**\
Address list with labels.

**Example:**

```
ethereum:0xabc123 (Treasury)
ethereum:0xdef456 (Mint Authority)
ethereum:0xghi789 (Custodian)
```

**Answer:**

***

## 3. Asset Classification

#### Category

**Definition:**\
The type of real-world asset being tokenized.

**What to provide:**\
One of the following categories (or closest match):

**Examples:**

* US Treasuries
* Private Credit
* Corporate Bonds
* Real Estate
* Commodities
* Carbon Credits & Environmental Assets
* Equities
* Stablecoins (Fiat-backed)
* Structured Products
* Funds / ETFs

**Answer:**

***

## 4. Market Data

**Definition:**\
Used to fetch live token price.

**What to provide:**

If listed on CoinGecko:

Provide CoinGecko ID

`usd-coin`\
`ondo-us-dollar-yield`

If NOT listed:

## 6. Proof & Verification

#### Attestation

**Definition:**\
Proof that the underlying assets exist and are properly backed.

**What to provide:**

One of the following:

* Attestation report link
* Audit report
* Custodian verification
* Proof of reserves

**Example:**\
Link to the attestation

If none:

Write:\
`None`

**Answer:**

***

## 7. Exchange & Access

#### CEX Listing

**Definition:**\
Whether the token is listed on centralized exchanges.

**What to provide:**

`TRUE` or `FALSE`

If TRUE, also provide exchange names.

**Example:**\
`TRUE – Listed on Coinbase, Kraken`

**Answer:**

***

#### KYC to Mint

**Definition:**\
Whether KYC is required to mint/redeem tokens with issuer.

**What to provide:**

`TRUE` or `FALSE`

**Answer:**

***

#### KYC Whitelist Required to Hold/Transfer

**Definition:**\
Whether wallet must be approved to hold or receive tokens.

**What to provide:**

`TRUE` or `FALSE`

This indicates permissioned tokens.

**Answer:**

***

## 8. Transfer & Custody

#### Transferable

**Definition:**\
Whether tokens can be transferred freely between wallets.

**What to provide:**

`TRUE` or `FALSE`

#### Self Custody

**Definition:**\
Whether users can hold tokens in their own wallet.

**What to provide:**

`TRUE` or `FALSE`

If FALSE → tokens must remain with custodian.
